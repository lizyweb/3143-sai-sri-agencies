Vettiver Products

Vettiver (Vetiver) products are renowned for their natural, eco-friendly, and therapeutic qualities. Derived from the roots of the vetiver plant, these products are widely used for their soothing fragrance, cooling properties, and versatile applications. Common items include vetiver mats, essential oils, handmade soaps, and body scrubs, all of which promote relaxation and skin health. Vetiver products are highly valued in aromatherapy and wellness routines for their ability to calm the mind, reduce stress, and purify the environment.
